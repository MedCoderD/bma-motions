UKMSC Deputy Chair (Education) 2024-26
23rd March 2026
MSC has commissioned a medical student survey in 2025 to gather views related to professionalism and fitness to practice experienced by medical students across the UK. Over 2,500 med students have responded and a report is currently being finalised and will be launched in spring 2025. The insights from this work will underpin BMA MSC's lobbying for clearer, fairer and more supportive professionalism policies.

Furthermore, MSC officers have presented the preliminary results of their survey and report to the Medical Schools Council.
